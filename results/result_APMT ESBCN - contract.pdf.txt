### 1. Summary of the Contract

The contract is a "Terminal Service Agreement" between APM Terminals Barcelona and several shipping lines, collectively referred to as "THE Alliance." The agreement outlines the terms under which APM Terminals will provide terminal services to the shipping lines for vessel operations including loading, unloading, and handling of containers. It includes specific provisions regarding payment rates, terms of service, responsibilities of both parties, liability, confidentiality, and conditions for termination.

---

### 2. Important Clauses

- **Termination**
  - *Quote:* “The Agreement may be terminated immediately at any time effective upon notice under the following conditions…”
  
- **Confidentiality**
  - *Quote:* “The Parties agree and undertake that they and their respective directors, employees… will treat and safeguard as strictly private and confidential…the terms and conditions of this Agreement...”

- **Payment Terms**
  - *Quote:* “The rates shall be paid in accordance with... The Terminal Operator shall issue an invoice for the provision of Container Terminal Services... The invoice shall be paid by the relevant Line or Lines within 30 (thirty) days of the date of invoice.”

- **Liabilities**
  - *Quote:* “To the extent loss or damage is caused by the acts or omissions of the Terminal Operator… the Terminal Operator shall be liable for any loss or damage to: Any Container Ship berthed at the Container Terminal… Any cargo and any non-standard Container…"

---

### 3. Risky or Concerning Parts

- **Liability Limitations**
  - *Quote:* “In no circumstances shall any Party be liable to the other Party for any indirect or consequential loss or damages incurred or suffered by the other Party…”

- **Dispute Notification Conditions**
  - *Quote:* “Any rejection of a payment in full or partly has to be brought to the attention of the receiving Party… latest within one month after payment was due.”

- **Force Majeure Termination**
  - *Quote:* “...either party shall be entitled to terminate the Agreement by giving written notice to the other Party, unless the Affected Party has during the existence of the Force Majeure Event taken steps… to overcome…”

---

### 4. Contract Duration

- **Start Date:** “This Agreement shall take effect as of the first THE Alliance vessel call in APM Terminals Barcelona, (the “Commencement Date”)…”
- **Termination Date:** “...and will continue until 31* March, 2022.”
