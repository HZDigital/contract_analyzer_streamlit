1. **Summary:**
   The contract is a Terminal Agreement between ECT Delta Terminal B.V. and a coalition of shipping companies (Hapag-Lloyd AG, HMM (Europe) Ltd., Yang Ming Marine Transport Corporation, and Ocean Network Express Pte. Ltd.), who are referred to collectively as "THEA". The agreement outlines the terms under which services related to the handling and storage of containers will be provided by ECT at the ECT Delta Terminal located in the port of Rotterdam. It includes important provisions regarding duration, charges, confidentiality, liabilities, and performance standards necessary for the execution of these terminal services.

2. **Important Clauses:**
   - **Termination:**
     "This Terminal Agreement shall be effective as from 1 April 2022 (‘Effective Date’) and shall remain in effect until 31st March 2027 (“Fixed Term”)..."
   - **Confidentiality:**
     "Parties undertake not to use or disclose any confidential information... without the prior consent in writing of the other Party/Parties, except as required by applicable law."
   - **Payment Terms:**
     "A Line shall pay ECT's electronic invoices in euro (€) within 30 (thirty) days after the date of invoicing."
   - **Liabilities:**
     "ECT shall be liable for: a. Any loss of or damage to any Vessel... while berthed at the ECT Container Terminal; ... unless ECT proves that the loss, damage... was not due to its fault or negligence..."

3. **Concerning Parts:**
   - **Bankruptcy Termination Clause:**
     "In case ECT becomes the subject of any voluntary or involuntary bankruptcy... the Lines shall have the option of early immediate termination."
   - **Limitation of Liability:**
     "In no event ECT shall be liable for: a. Loss or damage resulting from any latent defect or normal wear and tear or inherent vice; b. Any loss of profit including... any other type of consequential damage."
   - **Force Majeure Duration:**
     "Should any period in which ECT... is rendered unable by Force Majeure to carry out its obligations... exceed 90 days, ECT... is entitled to terminate the Terminal Agreement..."

4. **Start Date and Terminal Date:**
   - **Start Date:** 1st April 2022
   - **Termination Date:** 31st March 2027 (with options for earlier termination under specified conditions).
